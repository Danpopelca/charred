FIRE-CRAFTED DINING, ELEVATED TO ART

Welcome to Charred 1967 — where premium cuts meet open flame and timeless tradition. Nestled in the heart of the Jersey Shore, our fine dining experience is built around one thing: the art of fire. Every steak is seared over natural charcoal, unlocking rich, bold flavors that no gas grill could ever match. From butter-brushed lobster tails to perfectly aged ribeyes, our menu is crafted for those who appreciate quality, precision, and a little smoke. Whether it's a special night out or a memorable gathering, Charred 1967 is more than a meal — it's a ritual.

Why Charred 1967?

A Legacy of Flavor

Since 1967

Founded as a family-owned grill house, Charred 1967 has spent over five decades perfecting the art of fire-grilled cuisine.

Crafted Over Natural Charcoal

Every steak, tail, and filet is kissed by open flame — delivering that unmistakable charred finish and rich, smoky depth.

Elevated Dining Experience

Fine Ingredients, Fire-Kissed

We source only premium meats and fresh seafood, grilled to perfection for unforgettable flavor.

Elegant Yet Inviting Ambience

Modern design, dim lighting, and warm tones create the perfect setting for both date nights and special occasions.

More Than a Meal

Signature Dishes

BYOB - Your Bottle, Our Service
